About Annapurna Mess

Welcome to Annapurna Mess, where we serve authentic home-style meals prepared with love and care. Our kitchen brings you the taste of traditional Indian cuisine, made fresh daily using the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

We believe that good food brings people together, and our mess is more than just a place to eat – it's a home away from home for students, working professionals, and anyone who appreciates wholesome, nutritious meals at affordable prices.
